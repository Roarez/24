How to play: Click numbers and operators to do a calculation. When you click 2 numbers and an operator, a new number in place of the used numbers.
Number --> Operator --> Number = New Number!
You need to do 3 calculations to reach the desired result: 24.
There are 192 diffirent cards (48 easy, 96 normal and 48 hard). Click one of the difficulty buttons above to randomly choose a card from that respective pool. Have fun!
